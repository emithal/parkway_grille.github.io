OUR CHEF

Chef Dolan Lane is a Pacific Northwest native from Seattle, WA.  He has called Oregon his home for the past 20+ years.  During this time, he had the pleasure to be part of the “great food movement” in Portland, building relationships with local farmers and producers.  This resulted with him working directly with his own farm manager to plan the upcoming planting seasons. The Pacific Northwest provides so much inspiration from land and sea for him to call Portland his home.
